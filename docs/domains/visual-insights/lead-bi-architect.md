# Lead BI Architect — Visual Insights

> **Career Level:** L8 Bridge | **Domain:** Visual Insights (Power BI & Data Visualization)

## Overview

The Lead BI Architect sets the enterprise BI vision for Accenture's DATC practice and, by extension, for the clients it serves. You operate at the intersection of strategy and architecture: you advise C-suite and senior client stakeholders on BI platform direction, lead multi-project or program-level architecture governance, and shape how the practice adopts and commercializes new Microsoft platform capabilities (Fabric, Copilot, AI analytics). You are accountable for the architectural integrity of the entire Visual Insights portfolio — not a single project. You build the next generation of BI Architects through mentoring, knowledge programs, and thought leadership.

## Learning Goals

- Define and communicate the enterprise BI strategy: platform roadmap, capability investments, and build-vs-buy decisions
- Lead architecture governance across the BI portfolio: cross-project design reviews, pattern library, reusable asset catalog
- Advise clients at executive level on data-driven decision making, BI platform selection (Power BI vs. Qlik vs. Tableau), and Fabric adoption roadmap
- Drive Microsoft Fabric adoption at program level: governance, capacity economics, migration from legacy BI platforms
- Shape Accenture's BI Engineering and Enterprise BI Architecture capability offerings in the Bucharest DATC
- Build internal BI thought leadership: whitepapers, reusable accelerators, training programs, credential materials
- Influence Accenture's relationship with Microsoft and other BI vendors as a subject matter expert
- Develop people at scale: capability programs, community of practice, structured career development for L9–L11

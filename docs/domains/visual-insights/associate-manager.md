# Associate Manager — Visual Insights

> **Career Level:** L8 | **Domain:** Visual Insights (Power BI & Data Visualization)

## Overview

The Associate Manager leads a Visual Insights team or workstream, owning both delivery and people outcomes. You are accountable for end-to-end project delivery across one or more client engagements — from scoping and estimation through go-live and hypercare. Your day-to-day involves unblocking your team, managing stakeholder relationships, resolving escalations, and making architecture trade-off decisions that balance client requirements against delivery risk. You are also a visible practice leader: you contribute to knowledge-sharing, capability building, and the team's reputation in the broader DATC Data & AI organization.

## Learning Goals

- Own full delivery accountability for a BI workstream: scope, timeline, quality, and team velocity
- Lead stakeholder management at the project level: requirements workshops, status reporting, change management
- Make informed architecture trade-offs: when to use Premium vs Pro, DirectQuery vs Import, embedded vs shared capacity
- Drive the team's ALM maturity: CI/CD pipelines, automated testing, workspace governance
- Develop team capability: structured development plans, regular 1:1s, career coaching for L9–L11 team members
- Contribute to pre-sales and proposal activities: BI solution design, estimation, and pitch decks
- Define KPIs and success metrics for BI solutions jointly with the client business owner
- Understand cost management for Power BI Premium and Fabric capacity consumption

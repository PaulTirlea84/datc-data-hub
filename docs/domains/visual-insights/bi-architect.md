# BI Architect — Visual Insights

> **Career Level:** L9 Bridge | **Domain:** Visual Insights (Power BI & Data Visualization)

## Overview

The BI Architect is a technical specialist track role for people who are deep individual contributors in BI architecture rather than people managers. You own solution architecture across the full BI stack — from source system connectivity and semantic model design through report delivery and operational monitoring. You are the go-to person for the hardest technical problems: performance at scale, complex embedding requirements, multi-tenant solutions, and integration of Power BI with a modern data platform (Fabric, Synapse, Databricks). You define technical standards and architectural blueprints that the broader team executes against, and you represent Visual Insights in cross-domain architecture forums.

## Learning Goals

- Design enterprise-grade BI architectures: end-to-end from ingestion layer to consumption layer, documented as architecture blueprints
- Master Microsoft Fabric: Lakehouse, Direct Lake semantic models, Dataflows Gen2, pipelines, and capacity governance
- Design and implement complex embedding solutions: multi-tenant, A-SKU vs P-SKU vs F-SKU, service principal authentication
- Lead BI platform integration design: how Power BI connects to Azure Synapse Analytics, Azure Analysis Services, Databricks, Snowflake
- Define and enforce Performance & Scalability Design patterns: query folding, aggregations, hybrid tables, DirectQuery optimization
- Evaluate and adopt emerging capabilities: Power BI Copilot, AI visuals, AutoML in Fabric, semantic link
- Author the team's architectural decision records (ADRs) and serve as technical authority in design reviews
- Contribute to Accenture's BI Engineering and Enterprise BI Architecture & Engineering skill areas

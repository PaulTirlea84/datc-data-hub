# DATC Data Hub

Welcome to the **DATC Data Platform (DEVI)** learning hub — your guide to career development, competency frameworks, and learning paths across the Data department.

## What is DEVI?

DEVI (Data Engineering & Visual Insights) is the Data sub-platform within DATC Bucharest. We are part of Accenture's broader Data & Analytics Technology Center (DATC) and sit within the global **Data & AI** practice — a platform that helps organizations optimize and reinvent their business with Data & AI, ensuring responsible and impactful application.

DEVI operates across two complementary domains that together cover the full data value chain, from raw ingestion through to polished, decision-ready insights:

| Domain | Focus |
|--------|-------|
| **Visual Insights** | Front-end data delivery — Power BI, reporting, dashboards, data visualization, BI architecture |
| **Data Engineering** | Back-end data infrastructure — pipelines, cloud platforms, data architecture, storage, governance |

Visual Insights practitioners design and build the interfaces that translate data into decisions. Data Engineering practitioners design and operate the systems that make that data available, reliable, and trustworthy.

## Career Levels

Both domains share a common career level framework spanning L8 through L12, with specialist Architect tracks bridging into senior individual contributor roles:

| Role | Career Level |
|------|-------------|
| Associate | L12 |
| Analyst | L11 |
| Senior Analyst | L10 |
| Specialist | L9 |
| Associate Manager | L8 |
| Architect (BI / Data) | L9 Bridge |
| Lead Architect (BI / Data) | L8 Bridge |
